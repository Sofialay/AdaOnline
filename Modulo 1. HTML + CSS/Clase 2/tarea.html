A recent Pew survey found that 81% of American adults own a smartphone. Trying to live fully without one in today’s world might be as hard on your brain as just giving in and using it.

Why you feel like you can’t remember anything?

  1. Episodic memory is remembering “episodes” that have actually happened to you, such as your wedding day, the birth of your child, or what you ate for lunch.
  2. Semantic memory is remembering facts like the sky is blue, or Richard Nixon resigned in 1974, or I went to Greenbriar Elementary School.
  3. Procedural memory is remembering how to play the piano or drive your car.
